# `Credit card title text box`

Credit Card Title is text field component refer to the person who owns Credit Card issued by Bank.

## Validation 
- Field is mandatory
- Field is alphaNumericSpecial
- Field is Left Aligned
- Field is Disabled
- Maximum length is 26
